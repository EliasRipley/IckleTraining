ALGEBRA
Solve for x: 2x + 5 = 15. Subtract 5 from both sides: 2x = 10. Divide by 2: x = 5.
Solve for x: 3x - 7 = 14. Add 7 to both sides: 3x = 21. Divide by 3: x = 7.
Solve for x: 4x + 8 = 24. Subtract 8 from both sides: 4x = 16. Divide by 4: x = 4.
Solve for x: 5x - 10 = 15. Add 10 to both sides: 5x = 25. Divide by 5: x = 5.
Solve for x: 6x + 12 = 30. Subtract 12 from both sides: 6x = 18. Divide by 6: x = 3.

Simplify: 2x + 3x = 5x.
Simplify: 4y - 2y = 2y.
Simplify: 3a + 2a + a = 6a.
Simplify: 5b - 3b + b = 3b.
Simplify: 7c - 2c - c = 4c.

GEOMETRY
What is the area of a rectangle with length 5 and width 3? Area = length × width = 5 × 3 = 15.
What is the perimeter of a rectangle with length 8 and width 4? Perimeter = 2 × (length + width) = 2 × (8 + 4) = 24.
What is the area of a triangle with base 6 and height 4? Area = (base × height) ÷ 2 = (6 × 4) ÷ 2 = 12.
What is the circumference of a circle with radius 5? Circumference = 2 × pi × radius = 2 × 3.14159 × 5 = 31.4159.
What is the area of a circle with radius 3? Area = pi × radius² = 3.14159 × 9 = 28.27431.

FRACTIONS
What is 1/2 + 1/4? Find common denominator: 2/4 + 1/4 = 3/4.
What is 3/4 + 1/8? Find common denominator: 6/8 + 1/8 = 7/8.
What is 2/3 + 1/6? Find common denominator: 4/6 + 1/6 = 5/6.
What is 1/2 × 1/3? Multiply numerators and denominators: 1 × 1 / 2 × 3 = 1/6.
What is 3/4 × 2/5? Multiply numerators and denominators: 3 × 2 / 4 × 5 = 6/20 = 3/10.

WORD PROBLEMS
Sarah has 5 apples and buys 3 more. How many apples does she have? 5 + 3 = 8 apples.
John has 12 cookies and gives away 4. How many cookies does he have left? 12 - 4 = 8 cookies.
A book costs $15 and a pen costs $3. How much do they cost together? $15 + $3 = $18.
If 4 students each have 6 pencils, how many pencils are there in total? 4 × 6 = 24 pencils.
A pizza is cut into 8 slices and 3 slices are eaten. How many slices remain? 8 - 3 = 5 slices.

ADVANCED ARITHMETIC
What is 15 + 27 + 13? 15 + 27 = 42, then 42 + 13 = 55.
What is 100 - 45 - 20? 100 - 45 = 55, then 55 - 20 = 35.
What is 7 × 8 × 3? 7 × 8 = 56, then 56 × 3 = 168.
What is 144 ÷ 12 ÷ 2? 144 ÷ 12 = 12, then 12 ÷ 2 = 6.
What is (25 + 15) × 3? 25 + 15 = 40, then 40 × 3 = 120.

EQUATIONS WITH VARIABLES
If 3x + 7 = 22, what is x? 3x = 22 - 7 = 15, so x = 15 ÷ 3 = 5.
If 2y - 5 = 11, what is y? 2y = 11 + 5 = 16, so y = 16 ÷ 2 = 8.
If 4z + 10 = 30, what is z? 4z = 30 - 10 = 20, so z = 20 ÷ 4 = 5.
If 5a - 8 = 12, what is a? 5a = 12 + 8 = 20, so a = 20 ÷ 5 = 4.
If 6b + 4 = 28, what is b? 6b = 28 - 4 = 24, so b = 24 ÷ 6 = 4.

RATIOS AND PROPORTIONS
If the ratio of apples to oranges is 3:2 and there are 15 apples, how many oranges are there? 3 parts = 15 apples, so 1 part = 5, therefore 2 parts = 10 oranges.
If a recipe calls for 2 cups of flour for every 3 cups of sugar, and you use 6 cups of flour, how much sugar do you need? 2:3 ratio, so 6 cups flour means 9 cups sugar.
If a car travels 60 miles in 1 hour, how far will it travel in 2.5 hours? 60 miles/hour × 2.5 hours = 150 miles.
If 5 workers can build a wall in 10 days, how many days would 10 workers take? 5 workers × 10 days = 50 worker-days, so 10 workers take 5 days.

What is the square root of 81? The square root of 81 is 9.

ORDER OF OPERATIONS (PEMDAS)
What is 2 + 3 × 4? Multiply first: 3 × 4 = 12, then add: 2 + 12 = 14.
What is 10 ÷ 2 + 3? Divide first: 10 ÷ 2 = 5, then add: 5 + 3 = 8.
What is (4 + 2) × 3? Parentheses first: 4 + 2 = 6, then multiply: 6 × 3 = 18.
What is 8 - 2 × 3? Multiply first: 2 × 3 = 6, then subtract: 8 - 6 = 2.
What is 15 ÷ (3 + 2)? Parentheses first: 3 + 2 = 5, then divide: 15 ÷ 5 = 3.

NEGATIVE NUMBERS
What is -5 + 3? -5 + 3 = -2.
What is -7 + 10? -7 + 10 = 3.
What is -8 - 3? -8 - 3 = -11.
What is -4 × 3? -4 × 3 = -12.
What is -6 × -2? -6 × -2 = 12.
What is -15 ÷ 3? -15 ÷ 3 = -5.
What is -20 ÷ -4? -20 ÷ -4 = 5.

MEASUREMENT CONVERSIONS
How many inches are in 2 feet? 1 foot = 12 inches, so 2 feet = 24 inches.
How many feet are in 36 inches? 36 inches ÷ 12 inches/foot = 3 feet.
How many minutes are in 2 hours? 1 hour = 60 minutes, so 2 hours = 120 minutes.
How many hours are in 180 minutes? 180 minutes ÷ 60 minutes/hour = 3 hours.
How many centimeters are in 1 meter? 1 meter = 100 centimeters.
How many meters are in 250 centimeters? 250 centimeters ÷ 100 centimeters/meter = 2.5 meters.

STATISTICS AND PROBABILITY
What is the mean of 2, 4, 6, 8? (2 + 4 + 6 + 8) ÷ 4 = 20 ÷ 4 = 5.
What is the median of 3, 7, 2, 9, 5? Sorted: 2, 3, 5, 7, 9. Median is 5.
What is the mode of 2, 3, 2, 4, 2, 5? The number that appears most is 2.
If you flip a coin, what is the probability of heads? 1 out of 2 possible outcomes = 1/2 = 50%.
If you roll a die, what is the probability of rolling a 3? 1 out of 6 possible outcomes = 1/6.

ADVANCED WORD PROBLEMS
A store sells shirts for $20 each and pants for $30 each. If someone buys 2 shirts and 3 pants, how much do they spend? 2 × $20 + 3 × $30 = $40 + $90 = $130.
If a train travels at 60 miles per hour for 2.5 hours, how far does it travel? 60 × 2.5 = 150 miles.
A rectangular garden is 12 meters long and 8 meters wide. What is its perimeter? 2 × (12 + 8) = 2 × 20 = 40 meters.
If 3 workers can complete a job in 6 days, how many days would 2 workers take? 3 × 6 = 18 worker-days, so 2 workers take 9 days.
A recipe calls for 2 cups of flour to make 12 cookies. How many cups are needed for 30 cookies? 2 cups ÷ 12 cookies = 1/6 cup per cookie, so 30 cookies need 30 × 1/6 = 5 cups.
